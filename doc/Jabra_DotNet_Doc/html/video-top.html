Video Overview

Having a device with a camera allows several video related features to be accessible through the SDK. Some are simple operations like setting video parameters, panning and tilting the camera view and zooming. Other features are more advanced in that they let the device perform complex tasks and control them, e.g. streaming a whiteboard as a separate stream, or counting the number of people in the field of view, and warning if a limit is exceeded.

  1. Image Settings: Change picture parameters like brightness and saturation.
  2. View: Pan, tilt, and zoom to change the field of view.
  3. Limits: Get the actual allowed parameter limits for a specific device.
  4. Presets: Store and recall image settings and views.
  5. Whiteboard: Designate an area of the field of view as a whiteboard, and stream that in a separate stream.
  6. Video Stream: How to access the video streams from the device.
  7. Preparations: The steps required to use the video features.
